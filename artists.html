Pari's Top 10 Favorite Artists

A curated collection of some of my favorite and most played artists.

1. ENHYPEN

My favorite artist of all time. Their unique concept and versatile discography make them the group I return to the most.

View ENHYPEN Spotlight! →

2. BIGBANG

A childhood icon of mine, BIGBANG’s impact on K-pop and their genre-blending sound make them a timeless favorite.

3. DEAN

A staple in the K-R&B world, Dean's smooth sound and emotional lyrics tug at my heartstrings.

4. SEVENTEEN

I've been a fan of SEVENTEEN since high school. Their self-produced music and energetic performance style makes their work very engaging.

5. LE SSERAFIM

LE SSERAFIM’s confident, feminine, experimental sound makes every release instantly addictive.

6. BTS

A classic in the K-Pop scene. Their journey to global fame has shaped their extensive discography.

7. NEWJEANS

With one of the catchiest discographes in K-Pop, NewJeans’ nostalgic, refreshing sound is impossible not to loop on repeat.

8. TWICE

TWICE’s bright, catchy music sets the vibe. Their growth from a cute to a powerful, empowering concept is iconic.

9. YOASOBI

YOASOBI’s storytelling approach makes every song feel like a tiny emotional movie.

10. WAVE TO EARTH

Wave to Earth’s dreamy indie sound is calm, soft, and perfect for late-night listening.
